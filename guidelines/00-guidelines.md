# Format Policy Registry: Guidelines
This section of the FPR provides a general discussion of file formats and the challenges they pose for long-term preservation of accessible digital materials. It outlines general strategies and SFU Archives' current approach. And it provides a fuller explanation of some of the key terms used in the FPR's [list of preferred and acceptable file formats](../fpr/00-fpr.md).
